
# Trade By

### Starting formation

Trade By

### Command examples

Trade By

### Dance Action

Centers Pass Thru while Outsides, who must be Couples facing out, Partner Trade

### Ending formation

Eight Chain Thru

### Timing

4

### Styling

Same as Pass Thru and Partner Trade.

### Comment

The Ocean Wave Rule applies to the center dancers (e.g., from a 3/4 Tag)

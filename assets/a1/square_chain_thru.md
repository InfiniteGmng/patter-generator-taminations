
# Square Chain Thru

Timing: 14

From facing couples:
***All Right [Pull By](../b1/pull_by.md),
[Quarter In](quarter_in.md)***,
***[Left Swing Thru](../b2/swing_thru.md)***,
and ***Left [Turn Thru](../ms/turn_thru.md)***,
to end in couples back to back.

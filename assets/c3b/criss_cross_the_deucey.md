
# Criss Cross The Deucey

From Parallel Waves, applicable 2 x 4 T-Bones, or the formation obtained
after All 4 Couples Touch 1/4.

Trailers [Cross Extend](../c1/cross_extend.md),
Arm Turn 3/4 and Spread as Leaders 1/2 Split Circulate and then Phantom Crossover Circulate.

Ends in Parallel Waves, Point-to-Point Diamonds, or a Thar.

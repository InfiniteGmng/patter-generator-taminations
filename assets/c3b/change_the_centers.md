
# Change the Centers | Wave

From a Wave or Facing Couples.

**Change The Centers:**  
***Arm Turn 1/2***;
***Slip***;
***Centers Cross Run***;
***__Slip__***.

**Change The Wave:**  
***Arm Turn 1/2***;
***Slip***;
***Centers Cross Run***;
***__Swing__***.

Both calls end in a Wave and are 4-part calls.

All Eight Recycle

From a 1/4 Tag, or Wave between Parallel Mini-Waves: The centers do a Recycle, working wider than normal, and ending on the outside. Meanwhile, the outsides move into the center and Recycle (Facing Couples Recycle or Split Recycle, as appropriate), ending in the center. This ends in a 1/4 Tag.


# Scoot and Little

From quarter tag:
***[Scoot Back](../ms/scoot_back.md),*** then
***[Little](little.md) or Left Little***,
according to the hand with which
the Scoot Back was done, or outsides go as directed, as in
"Scoot and Little, Out on the outside".

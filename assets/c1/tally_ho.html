Tally Ho

From Parallel Waves or Lines, Trade-By, and 2x4 T-Bones: All 1/2 Circulate. The centers Hinge and 1/2 Box Circulate, while the outsides Trade. Those who meet Cast 3/4 while the other four dancers Hourglass Circulate, to form Parallel Waves or Lines.


In the second example the dancers doing the Hourglass Circulate come to the same spot and take right hands.

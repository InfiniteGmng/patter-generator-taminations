
# Ramble

From one-quarter tag or three-quarter tag:
***The outsides separate*** and
***[Slide Thru](../ms/slide_thru.md)*** with each other, while
***the centers [Single Wheel](../a2/single_wheel.md)*** and
***[Slide Thru](../ms/slide_thru.md)***.

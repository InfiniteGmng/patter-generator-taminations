
# Detour

From any 2x4 formation with ends in Tandem (or equivalent formations, like
Diamonds): Center four [Counter Rotate 1/4](../a2/box_counter_rotate.md), 
while ends 1/2 [Zoom](../b2/zoom.md) & [Hinge](../ms/hinge.md).

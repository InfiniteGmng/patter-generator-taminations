
# Rims / Hubs Trade \<anything>

## Rims Trade \<anything>

From Parallel Waves or Lines, a Thar Star, or Promenade:
***All [Partner Trade](../b2/trade.md)***, then
***the original Outsides do the \<anything> call***.

## Hubs Trade \<anything>

From Parallel Waves or Lines, a Thar Star, or Promenade:
***All [Partner Trade](../b2/trade.md)***, then
***the original Centers do the \<anything> call***.


# Follow to a Diamond

From a Mini-Wave Box or applicable 2x2 T-Bone: Trailers Follow
Your Neighbor and Spread as Leaders Box Circulate once and a half. A
Mini-Wave Box ends in a Diamond, a 2x2 T-Bone usually ends in a Wave.

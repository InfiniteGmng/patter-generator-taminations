
# Lockers Choice
**Parts:** 3  



From Parallel Waves or other applicable formations:
Lockit; Center Line Lockit as Others Trade;
Center Line Hinge as Others Step and Fold. Parallel Waves end in opposite-handed Parallel Waves.

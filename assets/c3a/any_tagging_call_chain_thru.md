
# <tag> Chain Thru

**Parts:** 2  

Starting formation is determined by the <tag> action.
Do the designated type of <tag> action
(e.g., "Flip", "Tag", "Vertical Tag")
to the 1/2 Tag position; Scoot Chain Thru. Ends in Parallel Waves


# Follow Your Leader
## Follow Your Leader

From Parallel Waves: Leaders 1/2 Split Circulate and Tandem
Cross Fold as Trailers Follow Your Neighbor (Extend and Arm Turn
3/4); all Tandem Extend. Ends in Columns.
